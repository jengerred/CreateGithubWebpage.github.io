How to create a webpage using GitHub

Step 1

Sign up for GitHub. (Don't worry it's free!) Go to github.com


Step 2

Create a new repository. You can do this by clicking the "repositories" tab and then click "new" to add a new repository. Your new repository is basically a folder to hold all of your files for the webpage you are creating.


Step 3

Give your repository a name. There is a box labeled "repository name" type the name you want to give it there. This will be the name of your webpage.


Step 4

Now you will need to add your files. Click the "upload files" button. You can drag and drop files, such as images or you can click "choose your files" to upload your files.
*Make sure you have an index.html file.


Step 5

Click the green "commit changes" button at the bottom of page to commit your files


Step 6

Add envoronment by clicking "settings" at top right of page. Then click "Envoronments" on left side menu. Click "new environment" and give it a name. Then click "configure environment".


Step 7

Now build and deploy your webpage by clicking "pages" on the left menu. Then under branch check that it says main.

That's it! Now check your new web page by cicking the "github pages" link under the "environments" tab on the bottom right of your repository page. Then click "view deployment".
